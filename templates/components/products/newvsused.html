The advantages of new tires are that they are brand new and covered under warranties by the manufacturer and some even offer road hazard warranties. Though with these bells and whistles comes the high dollar amount which can be detracting and not so ideal when in a financial pinch or when you leased the vehicle.

With used tires the disadvantage is that they are not new and are not covered by a manufacturer warranty but, advantage of going used is that it is lighter on your wallet which the extra money saved can be put towards labor or installation costs. Also if you're currently leasing your vehicle you wouldn't want to pay for brand new tires to end up not even using the tires you're investing in. Used tires are an eco-friendly cost-wise alternative that are still great for the road and fits the bill.
