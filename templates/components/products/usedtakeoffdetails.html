Our Inspection Process

We will do a visual inspection as well as a pressure test to each and every single one of our tires when it arrives.

We will repair any damage to R.M.A.(Rubber Manufacturers Association) guidelines.

That means:

  • The hole must be in the tread section of the tire
  • The hole must not exceeding 6mm in diameter
  • It must be repaired using both a stem and patch
  • We will NOT sell tires manufactured before 2011
